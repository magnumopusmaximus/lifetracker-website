🔒 LifeTracker - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Data We Collect

LifeTracker collects ONLY data that you voluntarily enter:

  • Workouts (Stamina & Strength entries)
  • Habits tracking
  • Mood journal entries
  • Financial data
  • Custom tracker data
  • Language preference

2. Where Your Data is Stored

  • ✅ Locally on YOUR device (localStorage)
  • ✅ NOT stored on our servers
  • ✅ NOT shared with third parties
  • ✅ You have complete control

3. Automatic Data Collection

Minimal technical data only:

  • Browser type & version (for compatibility)
  • Operating system
  • Timestamps of activities

4. What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Location data (GPS)
  • ❌ Contact information
  • ❌ Credit card data
  • ❌ Biometric data
  • ❌ Device identifiers

5. Ad Network (AdMob)

LifeTracker uses Google AdMob to show ads:

  • Google may collect data for ad personalization
  • View Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can manage ad personalization in your Google Account settings

6. Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Access your data
  • ✅ Delete your data
  • ✅ Export your data
  • ✅ Refuse data processing

To exercise your rights, contact us at: support@lifetracker.app

7. Security

  • ✅ HTTPS encryption
  • ✅ Data encryption in transit
  • ✅ Regular security checks
  • ✅ Local storage (maximum control)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of LifeTracker means you accept the new policy.
